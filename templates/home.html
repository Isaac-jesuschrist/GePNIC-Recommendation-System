About GePNIC

National Informatics Centre (NIC), Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India has developed eProcurement software system, GePNIC to cater to the procurement/tendering requirements of the Government departments and organizations. GePNIC was launched in 2007 and was matured as a product over the decade. The system is Generic in nature and can easily be adapted for all kinds of procurement activities such as Goods, Services and Works by accross government.
